How to Calibrate

  1. Place any object with a known dimension next to your bakery item. A credit card (85.6mm wide), a ruler, or a printed square all work.
  2. Enter the known dimension below.
  3. Go to Capture, tap the ruler icon (bottom right), then tap two points on the reference object spanning that known distance.
  4. Scale is set. Now capture items normally.
mm

Default 85.6mm = credit card width. Change to match your reference object.

Tips for Best Results

Use a white or light surface for maximum contrast. Keep the phone 12 to 18 inches above the item, as straight down as possible. Consistent, even lighting reduces errors significantly.
