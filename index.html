How It Works

  1. Print a dark square marker and place it on your white background near the item.
  2. Set the marker size above to match your printed square.
  3. Tap Capture. The marker is auto-detected and scale is computed.
  4. Tap the item you want measured. Done.

Tips for Best Results

Use a white or light surface. Keep the phone 12 to 18 inches above, as straight down as possible. Make sure the full marker is visible in frame. Consistent, even lighting reduces errors.
